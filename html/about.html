About Me

Hi! I’m Nick, an aspiring programmer from Australia looking to broaden my skill-set in all aspects of programming. I’m currently learning C# in hope to aid in my future ambition as a game developer. I’ve also started to dabble in Linux on the side.

I was first introduced to programming through HTML when I wanted to learn how websites were made. While I only learnt the basics, this sparked my interest in other programming languages. I later took Computer Science as one of my courses in year 12 learning Java, and I absolutely loved it.

My first personal project was setting up an emulation machine using RetroPie on a Raspberry Pi. I got everything working on a Pi Zero W and later upgraded to a 3b+ with a NESPi case. In the future I hope to implement my own code into these types of projects.

I hope you enjoy looking through my site and maybe you’ll even learn something!
